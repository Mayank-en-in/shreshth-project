The Online Games Showcase, meticulously curated by Shreshth Singh, embodies his dedication and passion for the world of non-violent online gaming. Serving as the project owner, Shreshth envisioned a platform that not only entertains but also advocates for positive gaming experiences. Moshin Sir, the guiding teacher, played a pivotal role in providing valuable insights and creating an environment conducive to creativity and learning. This dynamic duo's collaborative efforts have resulted in an engaging space that celebrates and promotes the diverse landscape of non-violent online gaming. Their combined vision and expertise make the showcase a valuable resource, fostering an enjoyable and accessible gaming experience for enthusiasts of all ages.
